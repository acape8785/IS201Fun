Talents

What are my special talents?
  1. Musical Theater
    • I began my passion for musical theater during my 3rd grade year when I performed in my first musical "Alice in Wonderland," playing the role of small Alice. After that wonderful experience, I decided to continue pursuing musical theater for many years to follow. I have partecipated in numerous other theatrical productions, including several musicals during my high school years where I was cast in leading roles. In addition, I have extensive years of training and skill development in the areas of singing and acting.
  2. Competitive Dance
    • Since the age of 3, dance has played a pivotal role in my life. I have taken many classes throughout my life, and I currently take dance courses at BYU. Alongside the various dance classes that I have taken, I have performed and competed with many dance companies. Although I have experience in most of the main dance styles, my favorite genres are ballet and jazz. Additionally, I even utilize my dance skills in my musical theater pursuits because many musicals employ dancing in the musical numbers.
  3. Art
    • Although my art skills are currently rusty, there was a time when I considered myself a gifted artist. My interest in art began during my middle school years, and it continued as I took several art classes in high school. I have experience with a few different mediums, but oddly enough, my favorite is colored pencil. Perhaps my greatest creation was in my honors art class in high school. I created a set of 6 correlated works of art, each portraying a different natural landscape.
