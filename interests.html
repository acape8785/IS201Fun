Interests

What are my interests?

I enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, ziplining, repelling, offroading, jet-skiing, and many other high-adventure activities. In addition, I love traveling to and exploring far away places with my family and friends. I especially love experiencing new cultures and countries.

I find great joy spending time with my family, including my extended family members. I love attending and partecipating in family activities and reunions because my family is the most important part of my life. From simple at-home activities to extensive recreational activites, I value every memory that I make and every experience that I have with my family!

As illustrated by my aforementioned talents, music plays a big role in my life. Although my favorite genre is indie/folk pop, I appreciate songs and artists from many different styles of music. However, I dislike most country and rap songs. Aside from the etertainment aspect of music, I often listen to music to relieve stress and allow myself to feel my emotions.
